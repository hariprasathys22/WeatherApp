QUESTION 02
Time spent 2s
Incorrect 0

Which of the following is C’s day off?

Correct Answer:

Wednesday

This question can be solved by tabulating the given information in the form of a table.

Now, as we solve this step by step. Kindly record your responses in the table.

Step 1: B does not work in the same shift with C. Hence, B cannot work in shift II. Also, as only E and F work in shift III, it is obvious that B works in shift I. Now people of shift I cannot have Wednesday or Friday off. Hence, we can conclude that B’s day off should be on Saturday or Monday (Tuesday and Sunday have already been taken by A and D, respectively).

Step 2: A’s day off is Tuesday and he does not work in the same shift with either B or G. This implies that A cannot work in shift I (as B works in I). Also, only E and F work in shift III. This says that A works in shift II. Also, as A and G cannot work in the same shift, G cannot work in shift II. Also, shift III is available only for E and F. Hence, G works in shift I. This tells us that G cannot take a day off on Wednesday or Friday. Hence, G should take a day off on either Thursday or Saturday.

Step 3: F’s day off is immediate next day of C’s day off.The possible combinations for C and F, respectively, are Wednesday, Thursday or Thursday, Friday or Friday, Saturday. If C’s day off is on Friday, we will have F’s day off on Saturday. This dictates G’s day off to be on Thursday and B’s day off to be on Monday. Now, Wednesday will be the day off for E, which is against the given condition. Hence, C’s day off is not on Friday.The same logic applies if C’s day off is on Thursday.

This leaves us with the only possibility of C – Wednesday, F – Thursday, G – Saturday, B – Monday and E – Friday.

From the table, C’s day off is Wednesday.
